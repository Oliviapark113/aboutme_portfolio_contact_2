About Me

My name is Olivia Park. I am originally from Seoul, Korea.
After finishing my undergraduate degree in Korea, I moved to New York City in 2002 to continue my education.
I graduated from the Fashion Institute of Technology in 2003.
Since graduation, I have worked as a fashion designer.
My primary design focus has been knitwear, activwear and dresses.
During my fashion design career, I have worked with a number of different compaies ranging from very large corporate compay to small boutique companies.
In my personal time, I enjoy gourmet cooking, traveling, movies and physical fitness.
I typically spend the summers being in the East Hampton with friends.
